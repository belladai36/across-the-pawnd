 ? HOW TO PLAY

Welcome to your interactive Pawnd

This is a two-player game for pups living on different shores. Visit whenever you can, interact with each other, and slowly build a shared collection of memories.

1
Choose your pup

Select your character on each device. You can give your pup a name and set its city, timezone, local weather, and day or night. Major cities are found automatically; missing locations can use a manually entered local date and time.

🐾🐾
Look for the two-paw symbol

Exactly seven tasks are live interactive games. They must be played together, usually while calling or chatting. Both pups confirm completion before the game awards two shared hearts.

✓
Completion matters, not correctness

Write your attempt and mark your half complete. Puzzles reveal their reference answer afterward. Creative games have no wrong answer.

★
Make one shared star

Both pups complete the same daily task. When both halves arrive, one star drops into the shared 50-day jar. A new task begins at 12:00 AM on the shore chosen as your shared Pawnd clock.

♥
Earn hearts and build a home

Regular task submissions earn one heart. A completed two-paw game earns two shared hearts. Each message or photo bottle earns another. Spend hearts on furniture and freely decorate your room.

✉
Send something across

Each pup may send up to five note or photo bottles per local day. They float visibly in the lake, and either pup can open any bottle. Each pup’s allowance resets at 12:00 AM on their own shore.

♫
Let the world make music

Turn on the instrumental soundtrack in the top-left corner. It changes with the page, daily task, selected pup, local time, and weather.

There are no streaks and no punishment. If you miss a day, the adventure simply waits beside the water.
